John Baez (Apr 29 2020 at 18:52):

The problem Jules mentions is a real one; it happened at the n-Category Café too, and that's why we (mainly Urs) invented the nLab.

John Baez (Apr 29 2020 at 18:52):

He and David Corfield pressured everyone who proved or collected really interesting facts to put them on the nLab.

John Baez (Apr 29 2020 at 18:53):

Other people complained about this pressure, so Urs left the n-Café in a huff and moved his base of operations to the nLab and its associated nForum.

John Baez (Apr 29 2020 at 18:53):

So he's basically documenting his research on the nLab.

John Baez (Apr 29 2020 at 18:55):

Some people complain about the nLab being hard to understand, but that's because it's serving a dual purpose: explaining lots of higher category theory, and also being Urs' "research notebook".
