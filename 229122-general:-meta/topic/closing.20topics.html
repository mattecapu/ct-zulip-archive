[Mod] Morgan Rogers (Apr 22 2020 at 16:31):

The problem with not being able to port topics across streams is that even if one does the next best thing of opening a topic under the same name in a more appropriate stream and politely directing people to it from the original stream, people still have a tendency to ignore the direction and respond in the original stream anyway, possibly resulting in a split discussion. In this particular case it's not much of a problem, since the topic in question is no more intrusive in #general than the Introduce Yourself topic, but I wonder if there is some solution, like the option to close a topic, to make redirects more effective.
