[Mod] Morgan Rogers (Nov 13 2020 at 10:49):

/poll Should Zulip go public? That is, should we:
Keep things how they are
Create a bot that one can message privately to receive a read only invite link
Create a bot that one can message privately to receive an ordinary invite link
Go completely public (no restrictions to becoming an ordinary member)
Go "read-only" public: allow anyone to view, but require one of the above options to become an ordinary member

[Mod] Morgan Rogers (Nov 13 2020 at 10:51):

Select options by clicking the boxes containing the vote counts. You can choose more than one option, so I'm going to arbitrarily say in advance that if the fifth option gets at least 60% of the votes of the most popular option, and that option is not the fourth option, then the fifth option shall be included as one of the new measures.

[Mod] Morgan Rogers (Nov 13 2020 at 10:53):

I shall also arbitrarily say that votes shall be tallied until 6pm CET on Tuesday (that is 17/11/2020). If anyone has a good argument for giving more time for voting, let me know.

[Mod] Morgan Rogers (Nov 13 2020 at 10:58):

Finally, if there are fewer than 150 votes tallied (counting multiplicities) by that deadline, the voting will be extended until the same time on Friday (20/11/2020) by default, to make sure we have enough folks' input. Voting will close at that stage. We'll implement whatever option(s) are chosen subsequently.
