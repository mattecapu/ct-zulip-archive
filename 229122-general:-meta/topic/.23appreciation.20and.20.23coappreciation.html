Matteo Capucci said:

Happy new year Eric! I'm not familiar with #appreciation or #gripes, what are they for

They are what you might imagine, for example, that :point_of_information: is an example of something that would go in #appreciation, it is basically acknowledging something publicly that you appreciate that is relevant to the community, e.g. you might appreciate someone hosting a series of web seminars, or someone who helped you break through some road block you were facing in your research, etc.

Gripes are the opposite. For example, if you are fresh graduate frustrated by employment opportunities, or some reviewer is being unreasonable, or if you are stuck on something, etc. Gripes often convert to appreciations when a solution is found. Often the existence of a gripe serves as an impetus for some improvement.
