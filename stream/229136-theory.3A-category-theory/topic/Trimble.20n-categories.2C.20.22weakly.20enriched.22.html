Topic: Trimble n-categories, "weakly enriched"

Patrick Nicodemus (Jan 27 2024 at 23:42):

I have been reading the n-lab page on Trimble n-categories and I noticed that a morphism of n-categories is "strict" according to this definition. So you have weakly associative and unital composition but a strict interchange law.

Patrick Nicodemus (Jan 27 2024 at 23:42):

Eugenia Cheng and Tom Leinster have given a notion of (V,P)(\mathcal{V},P)(V,P) enriched categories for PPP an operad on V\mathcal{V}V. If you could prove inductively that Trimble nnn-categories are enriched over themselves then it might be possible to modify the definition of Trimble nnn-category so that it is fully weak and so includes, for example, tricategories.

Patrick Nicodemus (Jan 27 2024 at 23:42):

One thing I thought of was that if C,D\mathcal{C},\mathcal{D}C,D are two (V,P)(\mathcal{V},P)(V,P) enriched categories then you could define a weak functor C→D\mathcal{C}\to\mathcal{D}C→D in terms of a function F0:C0→D0F_0 : \mathcal{C}_0\to\mathcal{D}_0F0​:C0​→D0​, and, for all sequences of objects x0,…,xnx_0,\dots, x_nx0​,…,xn​, a V\mathcal{V}V-morphism

Qn⊗C(x0,x1)⊗⋯⊗C(xn−1,xn)→D(F0(x0),F0(xn))Q_{n} \otimes \mathcal{C}(x_0,x_1)\otimes\dots\otimes \mathcal{C}(x_{n-1},x_n)\to \mathcal{D}(F_0(x_0),F_0(x_n))Qn​⊗C(x0​,x1​)⊗⋯⊗C(xn−1​,xn​)→D(F0​(x0​),F0​(xn​))

where QQQ is a kind of generalized operad controlling all the different ways that the functor FFF can be applied together with the composition actions in C\mathcal{C}C and D\mathcal{D}D coordinated by PPP. In my intuition, Q=P∘PQ = \mathcal{P}\circ\mathcal{P}Q=P∘P, i.e. an element of QQQ represents compositions in C\mathcal{C}C (the first P \mathcal{P}P), then the application of the weak functor, then composing everything in D\mathcal{D}D (the second P \mathcal{P}P)

Patrick Nicodemus (Jan 27 2024 at 23:43):

I am not familiar with enough differerent kinds of operads and I struggle to recognize a familiar definition here, QQQ seems like it is some kind of typed or colored operad. Does anyone recognize a known concept in this construction? In particular it would be nice if there were elegant rather than ad-hoc characterizations of the kinds of coherence laws QQQ should have to satisfy with respect to the action of P\mathcal{P}P on C\mathcal{C}C and D\mathcal{D}D.
