Mike Shulman (Mar 24 2022 at 17:57):

If the monoid is trivial, then this would be essentially trying to define a polycategory structure on Set\rm SetSet with ×\times× and +++ as the product and coproduct. It's known that that doesn't work to make a linearly distributive category, even though in that case +++ is associative. I'd have to look back at the argument to be positive that you can't get a nonrepresentable polycategory either, but it seems unlikely to me.
