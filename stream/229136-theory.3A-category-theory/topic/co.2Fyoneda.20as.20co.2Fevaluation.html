Let F:C→SetF:C\to SetF:C→Set be a covariant CCC-module ("copresheaf").
The coYoneda lemma is left extension along the identity: F≃∫cF(c)×C(c,−)F \simeq \int^c F(c)\times C(c,-)F≃∫cF(c)×C(c,−).
The Yoneda lemma is right extension along the identity: ∫c[C(−,c),F(c)]≃F\int_c [C(-,c),F(c)] \simeq F∫c​[C(−,c),F(c)]≃F.
