Topic: companion as "teleporting"

Christian Williams (Jan 11 2023 at 19:55):

A functor f:A→Bf:A\to Bf:A→B induces a "companion" profunctor B(f,1):A→BB(f,1):A\to BB(f,1):A→B (yes, we may have opposite conventions) whose values are B(f,1)(a,b)=B(fa,b)B(f,1)(a,b) = B(fa,b)B(f,1)(a,b)=B(fa,b).
