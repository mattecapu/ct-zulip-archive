Topic: Is Street's nerve fully faithful?

Daniel Teixeira (Oct 18 2025 at 12:57):

In The algebra of oriented simplexes, Street defines a nerve for strict ω\omegaω-categories via the functor Δ→ωCat\mathbf{\Delta}\to\omega\mathsf{Cat}Δ→ωCat given by [n]↦On[n]\mapsto \mathcal O_n[n]↦On​, the nnn-th oriental. This functor is a left adjoint by an usual Kan extension construction.

Street conjectures that ω\omegaω-categories are a reflective localization of sSet\mathsf{sSet}sSet via his nerve, i.e. that his nerve if fully faithful. The nLab says that it is faithful. Is it full? Anything I can cite on this?
