Topic: pullback of equalizers

Callan McGill (Sep 13 2022 at 16:19):

I recently was thinking about pullbacks of equalizers. If one writes the equalizer as the pullback of ⟨f,g⟩:X→Y×Y \langle f, g \rangle : X \rightarrow Y \times Y ⟨f,g⟩:X→Y×Y and Δ:Y→Y×Y \Delta : Y \rightarrow Y \times Y Δ:Y→Y×Y then (I believe!) equalizers being preserved under pullbacks follows from the "pullback cube lemma" - the pullback of a pullback square on each side of a cube is itself a pullback. Do you need pullbacks in the category for this result to be true?
