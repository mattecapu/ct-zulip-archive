Mike Shulman (Sep 23 2023 at 04:33):

I haven't thought about this before, but it's possible you might get something to work. The original construction uses the diagonals to define composition, taking the external tensor product of MMM over A×BA\times BA×B with NNN over B×CB\times CB×C to get M⊠NM\boxtimes NM⊠N over A×B×B×CA\times B\times B\times CA×B×B×C, pulling back along the diagonal to A×B×CA\times B\times CA×B×C and then pushing forwards to A×CA\times CA×C. But if you have the fiberwise products, you could perhaps first pull MMM and NNN both back to A×B×CA\times B\times CA×B×C (using the semicartesian projections), tensor them fiberwise there, and then push forward to A×CA\times CA×C (again using the semicartesian projections).
