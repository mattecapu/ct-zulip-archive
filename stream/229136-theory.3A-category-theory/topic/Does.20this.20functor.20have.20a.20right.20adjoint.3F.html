Topic: Does this functor have a right adjoint?

Chad Nester (Nov 25 2020 at 14:36):

I wonder if anyone can help me with this:

Let Prod\mathsf{Prod}Prod be the category of categories with finite products and product preserving functors, and let Lex\mathsf{Lex}Lex be the category of categories with finite limits and finite limit preserving functors. There is a forgetful functor U:Lex→ProdU : \mathsf{Lex} \to \mathsf{Prod}U:Lex→Prod. This UUU has a left adjoint, given by the finite limit completion of a category with finite products. Does UUU have a right adjoint?
