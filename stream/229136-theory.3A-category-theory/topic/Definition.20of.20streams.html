Nathaniel Virgo (Dec 25 2023 at 08:05):

I'd say both deserve to be called "streams", but personally I'd assume X↦A×XX\mapsto A\times XX↦A×X unless you said otherwise. There's lots of work that uses that definition, e.g. Rutten's work on "stream calculus".
