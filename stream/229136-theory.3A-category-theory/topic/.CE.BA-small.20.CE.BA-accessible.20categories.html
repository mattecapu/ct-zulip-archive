Mike Shulman (Jan 25 2023 at 19:07):

A small category is accessible if and only if it is idempotent-complete. Specifically, according to Theorem 2.2.2 in Makkai-Paré, it is λ\lambdaλ-accessible for λ=(#A+ℵ0)++\lambda = (\#A + \aleph_0)^{++}λ=(#A+ℵ0​)++. If κ\kappaκ is inaccessible, then #A<κ\#A < \kappa#A<κ implies λ<κ\lambda <\kappaλ<κ, so any idempotent-complete κ\kappaκ-small category is κ\kappaκ-accessible.
