Mike Shulman (Jul 14 2023 at 22:33):

If the whole adjunction lies in the slice 2-category Cat/E{\rm Cat}/\mathcal{E}Cat/E, which means that both functors make triangles commute and the unit and counit lie over the identity, then it's true because pullback is a 2-functor Cat/E→Cat{\rm Cat}/\mathcal{E} \to \rm CatCat/E→Cat and hence preserves adjunctions.

Mike Shulman (Jul 14 2023 at 22:34):
