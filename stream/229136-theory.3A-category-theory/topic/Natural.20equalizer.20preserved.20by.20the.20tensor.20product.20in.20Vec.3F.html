Todd Trimble (Jun 02 2024 at 12:27):

This should be the case for all vector spaces AAA, because every AAA is a flat kkk-module (a filtered colimit of finitely generated free modules: consider the union of its finitely generated subspaces). See Theorem 2.5 at [[flat module]].
