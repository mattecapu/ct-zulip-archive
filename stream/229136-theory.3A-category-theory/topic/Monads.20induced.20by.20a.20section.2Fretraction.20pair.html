I have the following conjecture. Let C\mathcal{C}C be a nice category, say a topos. Let s:A→Bs: A\to Bs:A→B, r:B→Ar : B\to Ar:B→A such that rs=id⁡rs=\operatorname{id}rs=id. There are pullback functors r∗:C/A→C/Br^\ast : \mathcal{C}/A\to\mathcal{C}/Br∗:C/A→C/B, s∗:C/B→C/As^\ast : \mathcal{C}/B\to\mathcal{C}/As∗:C/B→C/A. r∗r^\astr∗ has a left and right adjoint - left adjoint is just postcomposition with rrr, right adjoint is more complicated and relies on C\mathcal{C}C being locally Cartesian closed.
