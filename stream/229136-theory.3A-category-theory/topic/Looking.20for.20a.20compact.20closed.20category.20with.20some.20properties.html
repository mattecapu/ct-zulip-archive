Topic: Looking for a compact closed category with some properties

Jean-Baptiste Vienney (Feb 21 2024 at 21:06):

I'm looking for a category C\mathcal{C}C which is:

  1. compact closed
  2. has binary products (and thus binary coproducts by self-duality)

such that:

  1. products distribute over coproducts (i.e. it is like a distributive category but without requiring initial and terminal objects) (note that it implies that coproducts distribute over products by self-duality)
  2. theses products are not biproducts (i.e. it is false that for every objects a,ba,ba,b, a×b≃a+ba \times b \simeq a + ba×b≃a+b)
  3. C\mathcal{C}C is not thin (i.e. it is not a preoder)

Note some non-examples:

  • every non-trivial abelian lattice-ordered group, considered as a poset, verifies 1.1.1., 2.2.2., 3.3.3. and 4.4.4. but not 5.5.5.
  • the category of finite-dimensional vector spaces over a field kkk verifies 1.1.1., 2.2.2. and 5.5.5. but neither 333 nor 4.4.4.

Mike Shulman (Feb 22 2024 at 03:57):

Ok, I checked that the isomorphism is the coproduct injection. First, Houston's isomorphism A2+B2≅(A+B)2A^2 + B^2 \cong (A+B)^2A2+B2≅(A+B)2 has component A2→(A+B)2A^2 \to (A+B)^2A2→(A+B)2 given by "squaring" the injection A→A+BA\to A+BA→A+B and dually. And the distributivity isomorphism A2+2AB+B2≅(A+B)2A^2 + 2AB + B^2 \cong (A+B)^2A2+2AB+B2≅(A+B)2 is the left-to-right map also defined on A2A^2A2 and B2B^2B2 by squaring the injections and on 2AB2AB2AB by multiplying the two injections. Thus, the composite

A2+B2→A2+2AB+B2→(A+B)2A^2+B^2 \to A^2+2AB+B^2 \to (A+B)^2A2+B2→A2+2AB+B2→(A+B)2

with the injection on the left is also given by squaring the two injections, so it is Houston's map. Inverting the distributivity isomorphism we get the result.
