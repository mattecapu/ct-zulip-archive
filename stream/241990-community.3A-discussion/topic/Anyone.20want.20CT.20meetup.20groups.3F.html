Ryan Wisnesky (Aug 13 2024 at 16:08):

It isn't really a matter of leadership - anyone with a free meetup subscription can lead any group, and groups can be led by multiple people (meetup calls these people 'organizers'). It is a matter of who wants to pay meetup several hundred dollars a year to be the 'owner' of the group; basically, they just get the bill. So independently of who owns the meetups, I would encourage people here to do as they suggest, and try to re-invigorate the groups, which should be doable in their current state (certainly I can make people organizers of the two CT groups, lmk). Meetups can be online only, or in person; meetup is basically just providing the equivalent of a mailing list with a calendar to RSVP to.
