Rune Haugseng (Sep 11 2020 at 10:31):

If f∗f^*f∗ is a symmetric monoidal functor between closed symmetric monoidal categories, then some manipulation of adjunctions shows that the projection formula is equivalent to the natural map f∗[x,y]→[f∗x,f∗y]f^* [x,y] \rightarrow [f^*x,f^*y]f∗[x,y]→[f∗x,f∗y] being an isomorphism (where I'm using square brackets for both internal Homs). I guess this is what is hidden in the assumption that f∗f^*f∗ is a closed symmetric monoidal functor.
