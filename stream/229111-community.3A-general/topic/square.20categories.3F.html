I doubt that the above is existing terminology, but I think it is apt: has anyone studied double categories where both vertical and horizontal categories are the same? The reason one might want to do that is to put the focus on commutative squares as being the basic object of study by making them front-and-center. It is of course possible to study them in other ways, but that means picking them out (perhaps using some kind of predicate), which is awfully indirect.

Certainly one lesson that I've learned is that every single layer of 'encoding' that's done to study things tends to obscure the results somewhat.
