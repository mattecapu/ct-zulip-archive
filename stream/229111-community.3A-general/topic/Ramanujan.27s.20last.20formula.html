Yeah, I'd never heard of it either until recently! It's been proved, using sophisticated tools, that when xxx is real and 2x,3x2^x, 3^x2x,3x and 5x5^x5x are all integers, then xxx is an integer. It follows from the Six Exponentials Theorem.
