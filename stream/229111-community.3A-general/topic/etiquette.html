Todd Trimble (Apr 30 2020 at 19:32):

I like the list, and it would be nice to build an acrostic which gets the same points across. There's an acrostic endorsed by the National Autistic Society, SPELL, which gives a general framework for creating a supportive environment for people on the spectrum. If any of you know anyone on the spectrum -- family members, students, friends -- this can be an incredibly helpful set of principles to bear in mind.

Structure [consistency, safe and predictable environments]
Positive [approaches and expectations]
Empathy
Low arousal
Links [to patients, professionals, families, advocates]
