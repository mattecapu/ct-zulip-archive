Topic: Marta Bunge’s personal library

Ana Luiza Tenorio (Jun 02 2024 at 18:43):

It is with great pleasure that I share the following news with you:
The Institute of Mathematics and Statistics of University of São Paulo (USP) has received a spectacular donation of books from Marta Bunge's library https://www.ime.usp.br/en/professor-marta-bunges-personal-library-is-donated-to-the-ime-usp-library/

I completed my doctorate at USP and I am happy to know that new generations will be able to benefit from this material. It is an honor for the entire Brazilian category theory community.

On behalf of my doctoral advisor Hugo Luiz Mariano, professor and researcher in Category Theory at the institute, we thank everyone involved. In particular, we would like to thank @Valeria de Paiva for recommending USP. I believe this will greatly contribute to the training of more researchers in CT in Brazil and South America.
