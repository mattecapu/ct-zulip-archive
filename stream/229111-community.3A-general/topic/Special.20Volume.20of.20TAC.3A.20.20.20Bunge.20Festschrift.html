Topic: Special Volume of TAC: Bunge Festschrift

Valeria de Paiva (Jun 08 2024 at 07:44):

A special volume celebrating Marta Bunge's contributions to category theory has now been completed and can be found at
http://www.tac.mta.ca/tac/index.html#vol40.

The Festschrift, which was being planned and underway before Prof Bunge got ill and passed away in 2022, has 18 technical papers and a preface/postface with remembrances of Marta. The special volume editors Maria Manuel Clementino, Jonathon Funk, and I want to thank all the authors, reviewers, and the TAC Editorial Board, especially its managing editor Geoffrey Crutwell, for all the work producing the volume.
