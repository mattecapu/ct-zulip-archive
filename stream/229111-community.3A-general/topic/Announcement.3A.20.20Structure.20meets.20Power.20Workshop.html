Topic: Announcement: Structure meets Power Workshop

Tomáš Jakl (Apr 26 2021 at 09:26):

Hi All,
We're organising a little workshop (affiliated with LICS 2021) with the purpose of bridging the division between the semantically oriented ("Structure") and algorithmically oriented ("Power") branches of theoretical computer science.

Dates: 27-28 June 2021
Workshop website: https://www.cst.cam.ac.uk/conference/structure-meets-power-2021

The workshop stems from the joint project of Samson Abramsky and Anuj Dawar (called "Resources and co-Resources") where we use comonads and other tools from category theory in finite model theory.

Anyone interested in the broader topic of the workshop is welcome to participate/attend!
