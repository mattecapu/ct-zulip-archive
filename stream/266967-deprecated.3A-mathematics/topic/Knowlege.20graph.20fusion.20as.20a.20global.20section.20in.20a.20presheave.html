Matteo Capucci (he/him) (May 27 2021 at 09:13):

I assume you are regarding knowledge graphs as presheaves on small categories. Since the shape of the graph is not fixed, we are looking at the category ∫C∈CatPsh(C)\int^{C \in \mathbf{Cat}} \mathbf{Psh}(C)∫C∈CatPsh(C) (the category of elements of small presheaves), or more explicitly at the category of pairs (C,G)(C, G)(C,G) of a base category CCC and a presheaf G:Cop→SetG : C^{op} \to \mathbf{Set}G:Cop→Set on it, whose morphisms are themselves pairs (F,F♯):(C,G)→(D,H)(F, F^\sharp) : (C,G) \to (D, H)(F,F♯):(C,G)→(D,H) of a functor F:C→DF:C \to DF:C→D and a natural transformation F♯:G→F∗HF^\sharp : G \to F^*HF♯:G→F∗H (here F∗H=H∘FF^*H = H \circ FF∗H=H∘F).
Then fusion should amount to taking the colimit of a diagram in this category, a diagram describing the glueing procedure.
Global sections of the colimit should admit an explicit description, which I can't get off the top of my head now.
