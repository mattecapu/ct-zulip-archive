Topic: categories.pi-base.org

Tim Hosgood (Apr 03 2023 at 23:41):

a while ago I asked on math.stackexchange if there was something like pi-base but for categories, where you could search for categories by property, by name, or as counterexamples to theorems (check out how cool pi-base is for this if you haven't seen it already! you can search for things like "T5=>T4?" and see some spaces that are witness to the fact that this isn't true).

recently, one of the maintainers for pi-base said that if any category theorists are interested in building a version of this for categories, then to reach out. i would love to do this but don't have the time or energy right now, but figured that there would probably be a bunch of people on this forum that would!
