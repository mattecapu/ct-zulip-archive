If AAA is a right RRR-module and BBB a left RRR-module, then A⊗RBA\otimes_R BA⊗R​B is an abelian group. Moreover, if AAA is a left SSS-module and BBB a right TTT-module (compatibly with their RRR-structures), for any rings SSS and TTT, then this same abelian group A⊗RBA\otimes_R BA⊗R​B inherits an SSS-TTT-bimodule structure.
