Title: Lifting Algebraic Reasoning to Generalized Metric Spaces
Abstract: Universal algebra is the study of classes of algebraic structures defined by signatures and equations. Universal quantitative algebra is an extension developed to study algebraic structures over metric spaces (and generalized metric spaces). In an overview of this field, we will discuss quantitative equations, quantitative algebraic presentations of monads, and my hopes for a functorial semantics account.
