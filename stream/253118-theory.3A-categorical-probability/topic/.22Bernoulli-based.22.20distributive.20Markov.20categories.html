Stream: theory: categorical probability

Topic: "Bernoulli-based" distributive Markov categories

Sam Staton (Nov 03 2023 at 21:12):

Hi, We have recently come to look at a notion of "Bernoulli-based distributive Markov category". This is a Markov category CCC with coproducts and a faithful distributive Markov functor CN→FinStochC_\mathbb{N}\to \mathbf{FinStoch}CN​→FinStoch, where CNC_{\mathbb{N}}CN​ comprises the numerals 1+..+11+..+11+..+1. The idea is that we can have all kinds of spaces and crazy kernels in CCC, but on the numerals, it should be the familiar Bernoulli probability of FinStoch\mathbf{FinStoch}FinStoch.

Does it connect to other ideas?

Sam Staton (Nov 03 2023 at 21:14):

We used these in this paper (accepted at the POPL conference, conditionally). We have some methods for building them. From the perspective of categorical probability, the paper is about a correspondence between Markov categories of a certain kind and graphons. (We've been at it for a while, and I mentioned some preliminary results at the CPS workshop in 2020.)
