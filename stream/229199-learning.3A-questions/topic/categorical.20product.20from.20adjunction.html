Ruby Khondaker (she/her) (Aug 02 2025 at 10:49):

The relevant adjunction here is Hom((Z,Z),(A,B))≅Hom(Z,A×B)\text{Hom}((Z, Z), (A, B)) \cong \text{Hom}(Z, A \times B)Hom((Z,Z),(A,B))≅Hom(Z,A×B)

I.e. you can “package” two arrows Z -> A, Z -> B into a single arrow Z -> A x B, reversibly!
