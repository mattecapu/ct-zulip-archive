Ralph Sarkis (Dec 28 2020 at 11:14):

I think you do not have the right definition of pullback. For instance, in the first direction, you say

Suppose fmi=gjfmi = gjfmi=gj. To show that L+R is a pullback, we need to show that there exists a unique zzz such that fmi=fmnzfmi = fmnzfmi=fmnz and gj=gkezgj = gkezgj=gkez.

But that is not enough, you should show zzz is unique such that nz=inz = inz=i and kez=jkez = jkez=j.
