Enriching a category over a double category
Let D\mathbb DD be a double category, with V\mathbb VV the vertical category, A\mathbb AA the arrow category and H\mathbb HH the horizontal category.
One can consider, the usual notion V\mathbb VV-enriched categories and V\mathbb VV-enriched functors. Less usual, one can also consider H\mathbb HH-enriched lax-functors FFF between V\mathbb VV-enriched categories where the "laxity" diagrams are the obvious 2-cells of D\mathbb DD:

Lax double functors
In http://www.tac.mta.ca/tac/volumes/20/18/20-18abs.html , the authors describe a nice notion of lax double functor which is strict in the vertical category, strict in the arrow category, but lax in the horizontal category.
Those lax functors have a good notion of natural transformations making the whole thing a 3 category (that is what is claimed here : https://golem.ph.utexas.edu/category/2009/12/the_problem_with_lax_functors.html , if anyone know of a published proof I would be interested.
However, this structures is not even enriched, let alone enriched into a double category.

Double categories enriched over a category
Following the above intuition, we would like to enrich the horizontal category but not the vertical one. I was not able to find much literature and I had to build my own version (related to framed double categories) :
Let E\mathbb EE be a monoidal 2-category onto which we want the enrichment.
An E\mathbb EE-enriched double category D\mathbb DD is given by

  • an arrow functor D:W×W→E\mathbb D : \mathbb W\times\mathbb W \rightarrow \mathbb ED:W×W→E (The arrow category is then then given by the Grothiendieck construction on $\mathbb D$$ with the Grothiendieck projection corresponding to the source and target functors.)

(those 2-cells are oriented in the 2-category E\mathbb EE following the grothendieck construction but I do not know how to write 2-arrow in this library)

  • plus equalities of two cells for the associativity and the unity.

Putting everything together
These 3 constructions seems to behave quite well together by using V\mathbb VV and globular cells in place of E\mathbb EE above and enriching the diagrams of lax double functors.
This way we should obtain notions of vertical and horizontal D\mathbb DD-enriched lax functors, and D\mathbb DD-enriched natural transformations between verticals and between horizontal. I haven't checked yet, but I expect to have a triple category.

Do you know of similar constructions ?
