By using the Galois connection twice and functoriality of projection, we get

(aB)C≤(aB)C  ⟺  ((aB)C)B)A≤a  ⟺  ((aB)C)A≤a (a^B)^C \leq (a^B)^C \iff ((a^B)^C)_B)_A \leq a \iff ((a^B)^C)_A \leq a(aB)C≤(aB)C⟺((aB)C)B​)A​≤a⟺((aB)C)A​≤a

Then applying the Galois connection again we get

(aB)C)≤aC(a^B)^C) \leq a^C(aB)C)≤aC
