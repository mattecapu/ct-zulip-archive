Peter Arndt (Dec 03 2024 at 08:34):

If you take the language of usual intuitionistic propositional logic and consider not valuations in all Heyting algebras, but only in the Rieger-Nishimura lattice, you get a strengthening of intuitionistic logic, its so-called 1-scheme logic. See this article, the paragraph after Def. 3.1.

Peter Arndt (Dec 03 2024 at 08:36):

Section 2.3 also gives some hints on why to consider the ladder.
