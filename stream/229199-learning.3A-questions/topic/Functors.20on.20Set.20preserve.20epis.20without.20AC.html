Topic: Functors on Set preserve epis without AC

Ralph Sarkis (Jan 24 2024 at 14:04):

Do functors on Set\mathbf{Set}Set preserve epimorphisms (surjections) even without the axiom of choice ? (With the axiom of choice, all epimorphisms are split, and hence are preserved by all functors.)

Andrew Swan (Jan 24 2024 at 15:07):

No. You can still show that the epimorphisms in Set\mathbf{Set}Set are precisely the surjections. For each set III you have a functor (−)I:Set→Set(-)^I : \mathbf{Set} \to \mathbf{Set}(−)I:Set→Set given by exponentiation.
