John Baez (Jan 26 2021 at 03:44):

So UUU and U′U'U′ are spaces over BBB, meaning they'r equipped with maps to BBB, though of a very particular sort. Your equation is then saying precisely that your map f:U→U′f : U \to U'f:U→U′ is a map of spaces over BBB.

John Baez (Jan 26 2021 at 03:48):

So, you are describing a certain subcategory of the category of spaces over BBB.

The category of spaces over BBB is also called the slice category of BBB, or (more descriptively yet tersely) the over category of BBB. If you look at the link you'll see a commutative triangle, and this is precisely the equation you wrote down.

John Baez (Jan 26 2021 at 03:51):

All the business about the space UUU actually being an open subset of B×CnB \times \mathbb{C}^nB×Cn is too detailed and specialized to expect category theorists to have a name for it. :upside_down:

John Baez (Jan 26 2021 at 03:52):
