Topic: (co)limits of right adjoints

James Deikun (Oct 24 2024 at 05:05):

Let RAdj(C,D)\mathbf{RAdj}(\mathcal{C,D})RAdj(C,D) be the category of right adjoint functors from C\mathcal CC to D\mathcal DD. In the full functor category colimits and limits are computed pointwise, but what happens in this full subcategory? In particular I don't think right adjoints are closed under pointwise colimits.
