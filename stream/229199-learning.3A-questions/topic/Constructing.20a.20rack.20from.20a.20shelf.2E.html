Topic: Constructing a rack from a shelf.

Chad Nester (Jun 23 2026 at 12:21):

Hello,

I've encountered a binary operation that, somewhat unexpectedly, forms a right-shelf. That is, satisfies the equation: (a⊙b)⊙c=(a⊙c)⊙(b⊙c)(a \odot b) \odot c = (a \odot c) \odot (b \odot c)(a⊙b)⊙c=(a⊙c)⊙(b⊙c).

The potential connection to knot theory is intriguing, and I'm interested to know if it can be made into a rack in a sensible way.

Is anything known about this? I imagine there are general results about algebraic theories that I might look at, although I'm kind of hoping that someone has studied this problem specifically. It seems to me that someone here may know!
