Topic: spans for chosen class of pullbacks

Cole Comfort (Oct 15 2020 at 11:55):

Given a category X \mathbb XX, the category of "formal spans" in X \mathbb X X can be defined to have morphisms X1→Xn X_1\to X_nX1​→Xn​ given by a composable sequence of spans.

X1←A1→X2⋯Xn−1←An−1→XnX_1 \leftarrow A_1 \rightarrow X_2 \cdots X_{n-1}\leftarrow A_{n-1} \rightarrow X_nX1​←A1​→X2​⋯Xn−1​←An−1​→Xn​

Where composition is just formally pasting composable spans together.

If X \mathbb XX has pullbacks, the 1-category of spans in X \mathbb XX can be obtained by quotienting these so called "formal spans" by pullback. Suppose that instead of pulling back all cospans, one only has a class of chosen pullbacks in X \mathbb XX to quotient by. Is there a name/reference for such a construction?
