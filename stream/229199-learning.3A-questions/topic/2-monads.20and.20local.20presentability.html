Topic: 2-monads and local presentability

James Deikun (Apr 20 2025 at 19:31):

Does taking the category of pseudo algebras of a 2-monad whose underlying 1-monad is accessible in a 2-category whose underlying 1-category is locally presentable result in another 2-category whose underlying 1-category is locally presentable?

Nathanael Arkor (Apr 20 2025 at 19:51):

Assuming you care about the pseudo morphisms, then no, but such 2-categories do tend to be accessible. The reference for this kind of situation is Bourke's Accessible aspects of 2-category theory.
