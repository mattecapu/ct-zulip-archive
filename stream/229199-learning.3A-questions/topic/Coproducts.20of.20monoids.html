Oh right, expanding the syntactic construction, we can do the following:
Present a monoid MMM as an algebra TM→MTM \to MTM→M for the free monoid monad TTT (for TMTMTM the free monoid on the underlying set of MMM); taking the kernel pair of this map gives the congruence RM⇉TMR_M \rightrightarrows TMRM​⇉TM defining MMM.
