Nathanael Arkor (Jun 15 2024 at 11:48):

This is always possible. One way to see this is to observe that the (small) presheaf construction can be strictified into a 2-monad on the 2-category of locally small categories (see the references at [[free strict cocompletion]]) and hence restricts to a 2-functor Cat→CAT\mathbf{Cat} \to \mathbf{CAT}Cat→CAT. Then the assignment you are looking for is simply given by precomposing FFF.
