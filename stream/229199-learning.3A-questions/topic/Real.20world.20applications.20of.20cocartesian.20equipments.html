More precisely, two theorems from Structured and decorated cospans from the viewpoint of double category theory:

Let L : A ‚Üí X be a functor into a category X with pushouts. Then the double category
of L-structured cospans is an equipment.

and

Suppose A is a category with finite coproducts, X is a category with finite colimits, and
L : A ‚Üí X is a functor that preserves finite coproducts. Then the double category of L-structured cospans is cocartesian, hence also a cocartesian equipment.
