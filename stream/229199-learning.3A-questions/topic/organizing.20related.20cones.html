Topic: organizing related cones

David Egolf (Dec 01 2024 at 20:45):

Let D:J→[Cop,Set]D:J \to [C^{\mathrm{op}}, \mathsf{Set}]D:J→[Cop,Set] be a JJJ-shaped diagram of presheavevs, where JJJ is a small category. And let us assume that we have a cone under DDD with tip X:Cop→SetX:C^{\mathrm{op}} \to \mathsf{Set}X:Cop→Set.

Intuitively, for each c∈Copc \in C^{\mathrm{op}}c∈Cop, we can evaluate our diagram DDD at ccc, and get a diagram in Set\mathsf{Set}Set. So we should have some functor Dt:Cop→[J,Set]D^t:C^{\mathrm{op}} \to [J, \mathsf{Set}]Dt:Cop→[J,Set]. Similarly, by evaluating our cone at any ccc we get a cone under Dt(c)D^t(c)Dt(c) with tip X(c)X(c)X(c). So we have a bunch of related cones.

I suspect that we can organize the tips of these cones using a functor X′:Cop→[J,Set]X':C^{\mathrm{op}} \to [J, \mathsf{Set}]X′:Cop→[J,Set]. This functor acts on objects by sending ccc to the JJJ-shaped diagram constant at X(c)X(c)X(c).

Can we form a natural transformation from DtD^tDt to X′X'X′?

ιjc:D(j)(c)→X(c)\iota_{jc} : D(j)(c) \to X(c)ιjc​:D(j)(c)→X(c)
