Topic: Cylinder object model 2-category

Nico Beck (Jun 28 2023 at 12:30):

When KKK is a model category with a compatible Cat⁡\operatorname{Cat}Cat-enrichment , KKK has tensors and III is the walking isomorphism, will I∗AI*AI∗A be a cylinder object of AAA? I can factor the fold map as A⊔A→I∗A→AA\sqcup A \to I*A\to AA⊔A→I∗A→A, but I don't know how to check the other conditions.

Reid Barton (Jun 28 2023 at 12:39):

Yes provided that AAA is cofibrant and "compatible Cat-enrichment" is interpreted suitably (as in https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/enriched+model+category, for a suitable model category structure on Cat).

Reid Barton (Jun 28 2023 at 12:44):

To check the conditions to be a cylinder object, apply the pushout product axiom to: (a) the cofibration ∅→A\varnothing \to A∅→A of KKK and (b1) the acyclic cofibration {0}→I\{0\} \to I{0}→I of Cat, (b2) the cofibration {0,1}→I\{0, 1\} \to I{0,1}→I of Cat.
