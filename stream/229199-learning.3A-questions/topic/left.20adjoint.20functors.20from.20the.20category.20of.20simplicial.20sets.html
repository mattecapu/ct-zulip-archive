Topic: left adjoint functors from the category of simplicial sets

Leopold Schlicht (Oct 22 2021 at 16:59):

Is every left adjoint functor from the category of simplicial sets to a cocomplete category C\mathcal CC the left Kan extension of some functor Δ→C\Delta\to\mathcal CΔ→C along the Yoneda embedding? I found this claim in the title (but not in the actual text) of section 4 in this document. The title also seems to claim that there is a unified presentation of all left adjoint functors from the category of simplicial sets to any (not necessarily cocomplete) category C\mathcal CC (for which I can find no indication whatsoever).

Mike Shulman (Oct 22 2021 at 17:02):

Yes, because left adjoints are cocontinuous, and presheaf categories are [[free cocompletions]].

Leopold Schlicht (Oct 22 2021 at 17:58):

Thanks, so one just has to precompose with the Yoneda embedding to obtain the desired functor Δ→C\Delta\to \mathcal CΔ→C. I think it's a bit surprising that each cocontinuous functor sSet→C\mathbf{sSet}\to\mathcal CsSet→C is left adjoint.

Mike Shulman (Oct 22 2021 at 18:07):

Yes, the adjoint functor theorem is a bit magic.
