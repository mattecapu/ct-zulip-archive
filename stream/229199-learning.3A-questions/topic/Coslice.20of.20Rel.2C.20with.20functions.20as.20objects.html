Topic: Coslice of Rel, with functions as objects

fosco (Dec 14 2020 at 08:26):

Has someone studied the following bicategory?

Rel\sf RelRel is the bicategory of sets and relations; consider a fixed set AAA and the coslice A/RelA/{\sf Rel}A/Rel; now, (A/Rel)f(A/{\sf Rel})_f(A/Rel)f​ is the full subcategory of A/RelA/{\sf Rel}A/Rel whose objects are the relations A→XA\to XA→X that are also functions. Intuitively, it's like the coslice of Set\sf SetSet over AAA, but morphisms are relations R:X→YR : X \to YR:X→Y such that, for u:A→X,v:A→Yu : A \to X, v : A \to Yu:A→X,v:A→Y, one has R∘u=vR\circ u=vR∘u=v.
