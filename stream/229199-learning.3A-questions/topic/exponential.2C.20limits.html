John Baez (Mar 24 2021 at 17:26):

Vincent L said:

Is exponential a limit of a diagram ?

People here are exploring different ideas, but I think the best short answer is no.

It's true that exponentiation by xxx - that is, the functor sending objects yyy of some category to the exponentials yxy^xyx - is commonly defined as the right adjoint to product by xxx - that is, the functor sending objects yyy of that category to x×yx \times yx×y.

Limits are also right adjoints, but they are right adjoints of a different kind of functor.
