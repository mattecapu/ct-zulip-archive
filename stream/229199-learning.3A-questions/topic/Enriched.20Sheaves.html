Mike Shulman (Feb 01 2022 at 19:19):

In general it's tricky to say what you would mean by a "Grothendieck topology" on an enriched category. But one thing you can do is talk about V-categories that are left-exact reflective subcategories of presheaf V-categories. The resulting notion of "V-topos" was studied a bit in Garner-Lack Lex colimits, and probably other places.
