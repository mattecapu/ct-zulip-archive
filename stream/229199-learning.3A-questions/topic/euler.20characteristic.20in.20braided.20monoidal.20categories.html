Oscar Cunningham (Jul 07 2020 at 09:59):

There's a thing called a ribbon category

Oscar Cunningham (Jul 07 2020 at 09:59):

https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/ribbon+category

Oscar Cunningham (Jul 07 2020 at 10:05):

The idea is that in a braided monoidal category with duals you can draw string diagrams with the objects no longer being lines but rather being ribbons with some thickness. Then putting a loop in a ribbon is the same as introducing a twist
