Adittya Chaudhuri (Jan 22 2025 at 16:16):

I am interested in the 2nd approach mentioned in https://johncarlosbaez.wordpress.com/2024/11/14/polarities-part-5/, where a graph (a directed multigraph) is labelled by elements of a fixed commutative monoid MMM (coming from a rig RRR). They form a category LLL-Gph, whose morphisms are compatible with the underlying commutative monoid structure.
