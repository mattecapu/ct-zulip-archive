Idk about Kripke semantics, but if you have a measure space (X,F,μ)(X, \mathcal F, \mu)(X,F,μ), the topos of sheaves on F/ker⁡μ\mathcal F / \ker \muF/kerμ has an 'almost everywhere on F\mathcal FF' internal logic.
