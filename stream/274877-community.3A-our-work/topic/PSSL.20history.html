Valeria de Paiva (Oct 15 2022 at 04:47):

Another small bit of celebration for Dana Scott's 90th Birthday: the Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic (PSSL) is an informal meeting of categorists, type theorists and logicians, started in 1976 by Dana Scott, while in Oxford. There's now an nLab page, but we only have the titles of the talks till the 100th PSSL. We're missing at least 5 meetings and help providing webpages or listings of the talks for these missing meetings would be much appreciated!

Nathanael Arkor (Oct 15 2022 at 09:40):

There have been 6 meetings since PSSL 100.

  • PSSL 101: https://web.archive.org/web/20180831143433/http://www1.maths.leeds.ac.uk/~pmtng/PSSL/pssl101.html
  • PSSL 102: https://www.usc.es/regaca/pssl102/
  • PSSL 103: See message below
  • PSSL 104: https://mysite.science.uottawa.ca/phofstra/PSSL18.html
  • PSSL 105: https://sites.google.com/view/pssl-105
  • PSSL 106: http://www.math.muni.cz/~bourkej/PSSL106.html
