Ruby Khondaker (she/her) (Jan 18 2026 at 22:01):

Yes, that’s the sort of intuition I have. For example, I like to think of limits and colimits as packaging an entire diagram into a single object, such that maps into the limit represent “maps into the diagram”, I.e. cones over the diagram, and dually for colimits. You compress F-data into a single morphism.
