John Baez (Jun 29 2022 at 23:12):

Category theory is being used in a couple of somewhat different ways in quantum computing. For example there's the ZX calculus, based on string diagrams for symmetric monoidal categories, which I believe is used by the company Cambridge Quantum. And there's a lot of work on modular tensor categories for topological quantum computing, which is being pursued by Microsoft's Project Q.

John Baez (Jun 29 2022 at 23:17):
