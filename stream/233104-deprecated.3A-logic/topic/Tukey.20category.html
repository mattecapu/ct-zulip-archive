I think one way to see the extra ¬\neg¬ categorically is that the Chu construction is functorial on (lex closed symmetric monoidal) categories equipped with an object. So any automorphism d≅dd\cong dd≅d induces an automorphism of Chu(C,d)\mathrm{Chu}(C,d)Chu(C,d). When d=2d=2d=2 you can take negation as the automorphism, and then combine the resulting automorphism of Chu(Set,2) with its standard ∗\ast∗-autonomous involution to obtain your involution. I wonder whether it is also ∗\ast∗-autonomous with this modified involution, and whether Chu-theorists have studied it?
