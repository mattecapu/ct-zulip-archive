Mike Shulman (Jan 08 2025 at 01:35):

A commutative algebra generally means a commutative monoid in the monoidal category of kkk-modules for some commutative ring (or field) kkk, which is equivalent to a commutative ring RRR together with a ring homomorphism k→Rk\to Rk→R.
