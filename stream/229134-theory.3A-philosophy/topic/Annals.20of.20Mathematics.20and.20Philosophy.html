Jean-Baptiste Vienney (Mar 24 2023 at 16:47):

There is a new journal about philosophy of mathematics: Annals of Mathematics and Philosophy. So I want to advertise it in case you're interested by the subject and if you didn't notice. The first issue is available in open access here: Volume 1, number 1, 2023. Every single paper looks passionating, I think I'm going to read all of them! For example, three of them are titled "What makes mathematicians believe unproved mathematical statements?" by Timothy Gowers, a paper "Ways of thinking in mathematics" (Javier de Lorenzo), "What is axiomatics?" (Paolo Cantù). There is an interview of Penrose also and the other ones are attracting to me too.
