The universal differential d~:A→I\tilde d: A\to Id~:A→I

d~:x↦1⊗x−x⊗1\tilde d: x\mapsto 1\otimes x - x\otimes 1d~:x↦1⊗x−x⊗1 (Note: Bourbaki has a sign difference, but I will use this convention.)

d~:x↦[G~,x],\tilde d: x\mapsto [\tilde G,x],d~:x↦[G~,x],
