Steve Awodey (Jul 17 2021 at 15:40):

@Liam Chung you do know that Makkai is at McGill, right? And Joyal is at UQAM. They are both retired, but they are still excellent resources for CT, CatLog, etc. And there are many other top category theorists in town (and probably on this Zulip): Robert Seely, Bill Boschuck, Marta Bunge, Michael Barr, Gonzalo Reyes, ... If you are in Montreal, you could try to get in touch with some of these people and see if anyone is interested in advising you. And if you are Canadian (or even if you're not) you should look into studying at Ottawa, Dalhousie, or Mount Allison University. Check this out for example: https://www.mathstat.dal.ca/~selinger/atcat/
