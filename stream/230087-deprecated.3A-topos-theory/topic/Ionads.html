Topic: Ionads

Oscar Cunningham (May 13 2020 at 10:15):

In Richard Garner's paper on ionads he say that (aside from size issues) one can think of the sheaves on a space XXX as being the continuous maps X→SetX\to\mathbf{Set}X→Set where Set\mathbf{Set}Set has been made into an ionad using the Scott topology. But he also defines how to turn a category into a ionad using the Alexandroff topology. Does anyone know what the continuous maps X→SetX\to\mathbf{Set}X→Set are when Set\mathbf{Set}Set has the Alexandroff topology?
