Topic: essential geometric morphism

vikraman (Mar 27 2020 at 18:45):

A functor F:C→DF : C \rightarrow DF:C→D gives an essential geometric morphism between toposes C^\hat{C}C^ and D^\hat{D}D^, these are given by pre-composition, left, and right kan extensions. Is there a name for this construction, or is it written down somewhere?
