Topic: 3D commutative diagrams

Jean-Baptiste Vienney (Aug 15 2024 at 20:40):

Is there a software for writing 3D commutative diagrams?

Kevin Carlson (Aug 15 2024 at 21:39):

Homotopy.io is all about higher dimensional diagrams although more surface diagrams, which may not be what you mean. Asymptote is a programming language for vector graphics that has the best capabilities of anything for mixing LaTeX and 3D. But you have to learn a whole programming language. (It’s pretty similar syntax to C.)

Nathanael Arkor (Aug 15 2024 at 22:07):

What do you mean by "3D commutative diagram"?

I mean writing a 1-categorical diagram but with the shape of a cube for instance.

Nathanael Arkor (Aug 16 2024 at 06:50):

Tom Hirschowitz (Aug 16 2024 at 08:59):

You can also draw them in @Ambroise's editor — which doesn't appear to have any official name yet — by manually adjusting the ‘z’ level of arrows.
