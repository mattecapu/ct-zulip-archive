Topic: Open House on Category Theory 2021

Jens Hemelaer (Oct 07 2021 at 07:24):

On 18 - 19 November 2021 there is an event organized by Matías Menni and Luis Turcio:
"A short, informal meeting on Category Theory and an opportunity to celebrate Francisco 'Quico' Marmolejo's 60th birthday."

The event is organized in Ciudad de México but will take place online because of the Covid pandemic. For more information, see the webpage.

Regarding the topic, the website states: "Since we will be celebrating Quico's birthday we particularly encourage talks on Pseudo-monads, distributive laws, Toposes and/or Axiomatic Cohesion." Confirmed participants are Anders Kock, Susan Niefield, Robert Paré and Robert Rosebrugh.
