Topic: Call for Applications: Teaching Higher Category Theory

Nima Rasekh (Nov 06 2025 at 20:16):

Dear all,

This is the first announcement for the upcoming ICERM Graduate Training Workshop "Teaching Higher Category Theory with Computers", which will take place August 17 - 21, 2026.

Are you a graduate student (Master's or PhD) and want to learn about ∞-categories? Then we invite you to apply! Students with some familiarity with homotopy theory, category theory, homotopy type theory, and/or proof assistants who are interested in learning about ∞-categories are particularly encouraged.

Further details can be found on the ICERM webpage:

https://icerm.brown.edu/program/topical_workshop/tw-26-thc

Please submit your application via Cube:

https://app.icerm.brown.edu/Cube/apply/607

The deadline is March 31, 2026.

Best,
Nima, Emily, Jonathan
