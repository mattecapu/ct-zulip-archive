Applications for the 2024 CMU Summer School in Logic and Formal Epistemology are now officially open!

June 3–7
Francesca Zaffora Blando & Krzysztof Mierzewski
"Chance and Randomness"

June 10–14
Jonas Frey & Reid Barton
"Categorical Semantics and Synthetic Topology"

June 17–21
Clark Glymour & Kun Zhang
"The Logic of Discovery"

The school runs for 3 weeks, June 3–21, and is open to all undergraduate and early-stage graduate students. There is no tuition and on-campus lodging is provided free of charge for the duration of the program.
Applications close February 14.
Any questions can be sent to abjorn@cmu.edu.
