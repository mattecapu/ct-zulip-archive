Topic: ICRA 2022 Workshop on Compositional Robotics

Gioele Zardini (Jan 21 2022 at 10:25):

Dear all,

I am happy to announce our #ICRA 2022 workshop “Compositional Robotics: Mathematics and Tools”.

The workshop will feature an introduction to mathematical principles and practical tools for compositional robotics, as well as research talks.

If you are interested, join the official mailing list: http://eepurl.com/hQFTeP

You can find the detailed program of the workshop at the official webpage https://idsc.ethz.ch/research-frazzoli/workshops/compositional-robotics-2022.html

Furthermore, we officially released a call for papers related to the workshop, available at https://easychair.org/cfp/ICRA2022CRMT (deadline for submissions May 8, 2022).
Accepted submissions will have the chance to present at the workshop.

Thanks to the great team of co-organizers @Andrea Censi , @Jonathan Lorand , Emilio Frazzoli, @David Spivak, @Brendan Fong, @Paolo Perrone, @John Baez, Dylan Shell, Alexandra Nilles, Andrew Spielberg, Anne Collin, Matthew Kvalheim, Paul Gustafson, Daniel Koditschek, Aaron Ames
