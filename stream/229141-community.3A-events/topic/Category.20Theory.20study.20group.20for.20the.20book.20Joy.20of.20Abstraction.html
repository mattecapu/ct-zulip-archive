On the Applied Category Theory discord server there is a Study Group using voice chat for meetings discussing Dr. Eugenia Cheng's Joy of Abstraction book in synchronization with the official book club at the Topos Institute. https://discord.gg/hTEpgYv https://topos.site/joa-bookclub/ It is on Fridays at 10 am PDT or 1 pm EDT.
