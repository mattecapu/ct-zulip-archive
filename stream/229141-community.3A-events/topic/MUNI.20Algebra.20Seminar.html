Topic: MUNI Algebra Seminar

Alexander Campbell (Jun 19 2020 at 05:10):

Members of this chat room may be interested in the talk I gave yesterday at the Masaryk University Algebra Seminar -- which, despite its name, is a category theory seminar. In it, I introduce a new notion of double ∞-category, which I motivate by way of a new model structure on the category of double categories. Video, slides, and abstract are available on the seminar webpage.
