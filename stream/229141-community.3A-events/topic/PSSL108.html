Beppe Metere (Jun 21 2023 at 08:46):

PSSL108 in Sicily!

After the awesome edition held in Athens, the next Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic, numbered 108, will be held in Terrasini (35 km away from Palermo), on the weekend September 16th-17th, 2023.

The PSSL will be preceded by a thematic session in "Logic and Algebra" on September 15th, with amazing invited speakers: Maria Manuel Clementino (University of Coimbra), Vincenzo Marra (Universita' degli Studi di Milano), Zurab Janelidze (Stellenbosch University) and Enrico Vitale (Universite catholique de Louvain).

There is no conference fee, but registration is mandatory. Please register before August 15th, 2023, by compiling the form you find (together with other information) on the conference webpage:
https://sites.google.com/view/beppemetere/pssl-108

If you wish to give a talk, please send an email with the title of your talk and a short abstract to giuseppe.metere@unipa.it. There is no deadline, but please consider that talk proposals will be accepted as long as there are available slots in the PSSL schedule.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

We hope to see you in September!

Members of the OC are Beppe Metere (Universita' di Palermo), Luca Spada (Universita' di Salerno) and Tim Van der Linden (Universite' catholique de Louvain).
