1st CALL FOR CONTRIBUTIONS
PSSL 112
Nottingham, United Kingdom, 28-29 March 2026

We are happy to announce that the 112th Peripatetic Seminar on Sheaves and Logic (PSSL 112) will take place on March 28th-29th 2026 at the University of Nottingham, United Kingdom.

PSSL is a long-running series of informal gatherings traditionally held over a weekend in a European university. This meeting marks the 112th edition and it is the first to be held in Nottingham. Talks cover all aspects and areas of category theory and its applications.

Further details about the meeting, transport, accommodation and updates can be found on the PSSL 112 website. Below are the current important dates and information.

Registration
Please fill in the registration form, which can be found here, by March 14th 2026. The registration is free.

Submitting a contribution
If you wish to present your work as a contributed talk, please submit a one-page pdf (not counting references) with title, speaker name and abstract to the following email address: stiephen.pradal@nottingham.ac.uk.

Key dates to remember:

  • The deadline for the abstract submissions is February 6th 2026 (AoE).
  • The notification of accepted talks will be sent on February 16th 2026.

Talks about work in progress are welcome, and we encourage PhD students, young researchers and minorities to give a presentation.

We look forward to seeing you in Nottingham!

Stiéphen, Tom and Simona
