Call for participation

  • Aims and scope *

The event “Unifying themes in Geometry” aims to present and discuss fundamental insights and approaches to several key types of geometries, and significant links between them. More specifically, it will focus on intra-disciplinary connections between algebraic, arithmetic, tropical, motivic, p-adic, adelic, anabelian, topos-theoretic and model-theoretic geometries.
The school will feature lecture courses on different aspects of geometry as well as invited talks by top experts in central areas of contemporary mathematics.
It is our hope that this event will contribute to stimulate new developments towards a unified geometric vision shedding light on some of the most challenging problems in modern mathematics.
