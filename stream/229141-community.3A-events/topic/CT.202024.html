Dear colleagues,

The International Category Theory Conference – CT2024 – will take place from June 23rd to June 29th at the Universidade de Santiago de Compostela (Spain).

Submission of abstracts is now open. Please note that the deadline for submitting an abstract is the 20th of March. Notification of acceptance will be no later than the 30th of April.

We have also added some accommodation options on the webpage, with special prices for students. Unfortunately, availability is limited, so they will be booked on a first-come, first-served basis.

For more information about the conference, please visit our website at https://www.usc.gal/regaca/ct2024/

If you need to contact the organisers, please email us at ct.2024@usc.gal
