Yorkshire and Midlands Category Theory Seminar
Thursday, 20th June 2024
The University of Manchester, UK

Dear colleagues and friends,

The 35th meeting of the Yorkshire and Midlands Category Theory Seminar (YaMCATS) will be held at the University of Manchester on 20th June 2024. The speakers will be:

Marcelo Fiore (University of Cambridge)

Sean Moss (University of Birmingham)

Simona Paoli (University of Aberdeen)

Please check the YaMCATS website

https://conferences.leeds.ac.uk/yamcats/

for further information and registration. Please register by 13th June 2024.

This is a satellite meeting of the 75th British Mathematical Colloquium, which will take place at the University of Manchester from 17th June to 20th June 2024. See https://sites.google.com/view/bmc2024 for more details.

YaMCATS is a research network involving the Universities of Leeds, Manchester and Sheffield, supported by the London Mathematical Society.

With best regards,

Nicola Gambino, also on behalf of João Faria Martins (University of Leeds) and Markus Szymik (University of Sheffield)
