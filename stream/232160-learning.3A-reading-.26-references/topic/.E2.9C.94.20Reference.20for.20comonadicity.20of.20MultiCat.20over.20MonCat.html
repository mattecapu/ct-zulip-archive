Topic: ✔ Reference for comonadicity of MultiCat over MonCat

Quentin Schroeder (Aug 01 2025 at 12:23):

The KZ comonadicity of multicategories over monoidal categories is claimed on the nlab on multicategories in the section on relations to monoidal categories. I am aware of the papers: "From Coherent Structures to Universal Properties", "Representable Multicategories" and "A unified framework for generalized multicategories". But from searching through the papers I found no explicit mention of this result.

Is anyone aware of a reference explicitly stating this or something that implies this?

Matt Earnshaw (Aug 01 2025 at 12:50):

See 4.3 of Elmendorf and Mandell, 2009 -- with the caveat that they work with symmetric gadgets, which structure you can of course forget
