Topic: "weak" simplicial objects

Daniel Teixeira (Feb 18 2025 at 21:23):

The definition of simplicial object valued in a bicategory B\mathcal BB can be weakened by asking the simplicial identities to hold up to coherent invertible 2-cells rather than strictly. These conveniently match pseudo-functors on Δop\Delta^{op}Δop.

This idea is not new, I recall seeing an article in the 90s (perhaps by Street) which worked the details out. Would anyone know what I'm talking about?

Mike Shulman (Feb 18 2025 at 23:09):

Could you be thinking of "Fibrations in bicategories" (1980)?

David Kern (Feb 20 2025 at 01:14):

While it's not written for general bicategories, this sounds like Corollary 1.6 of Jardine's Supercoherence.

Daniel Teixeira (Feb 20 2025 at 01:47):

oh it was definitely this paper by Jardine! thanks Mike and David
