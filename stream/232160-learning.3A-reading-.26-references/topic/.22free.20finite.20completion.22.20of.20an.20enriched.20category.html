Topic: "free finite completion" of an enriched category

Ari Rosenfield (Jan 15 2025 at 23:57):

Hi, this is my first post ever! Does anyone here know of a reference discussing the free completion with respect to finite weighted limits of an enriched category (where the enriching category is sufficiently "nice;" i.e. closed symmetric monoidal, locally finitely presentable, etc.)?

Either an explicit construction or an argument for its existence would do.

Nathanael Arkor (Jan 16 2025 at 00:26):

The usual reference would be Theorem 5.35 of Kelly's Basic Concepts of Enriched Category Theory, which gives the construction more generally for a given class of weights.
