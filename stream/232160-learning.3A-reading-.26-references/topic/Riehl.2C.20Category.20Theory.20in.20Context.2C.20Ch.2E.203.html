Here are some questions you may find helpful to think over, for purpose of gaining intuition for colimits:

  • Is this partial order a total order?
  • Is there a smallest element that is at least as large as aaa and bbb? In other words, does the set {a,b}\{a,b\}{a,b} have a supremum?
  • In the category pictured above, does the diagram with just the objects aaa and bbb have a colimit? In other words, do aaa and bbb have a coproduct in this category?
