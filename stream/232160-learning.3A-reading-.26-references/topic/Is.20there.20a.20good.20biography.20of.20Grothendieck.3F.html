David Michael Roberts (Aug 01 2024 at 00:15):

Scharlau's books are good, I think. You can get the first one 'Anarchy' here as a pdf, and the individual chapters of the other one 'Spirituality' on this page. These go with with the middle volume, by Leila Schneps 'Mathematics' (four chapters available here). The first volume is mostly about Grothendieck's parents and his childhood, the last volume is about his post-IHÉS life, up to the 1991 disappearance. (These are all at the page Julius linked)

I just discovered that Scharlau died in late 2020, so I don't know what happened to what I thought was a planned fourth volume...
