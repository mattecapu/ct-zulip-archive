Hi Uroš,

For an elementary introduction to Category Theory with nice examples in the context of Computer Science, you may want to look at "Category Theory for Computing Science, Third Edition" by Michael Barr and Charles Well. It contains solutions to all the exercises.

Another nice introduction with examples from Computer Science and Logic is Awodey's book "Category Theory, Second Edition." It does not have all but many solutions to the exercises.

Yet, another accessible and superbly written introduction to Category Theory is "Basic Category Theory" by Tom Leinster. The book does not contain solutions to the exercises, but you may find those here.

Enjoy!

Francisco
