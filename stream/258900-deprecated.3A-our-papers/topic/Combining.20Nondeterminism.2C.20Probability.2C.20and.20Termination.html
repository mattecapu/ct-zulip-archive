Topic: Combining Nondeterminism, Probability, and Termination

Ralph Sarkis (Apr 01 2021 at 13:43):

I am delighted to announce LICS2021 accepted the paper Combining Nondeterminism, Probability, and Termination: Equational and Metric Reasoning by Matteo Mio, Valeria Vignudelli and I (this is my first publication :tada:). Our preprint is on arXiv.

We study monads resulting from the combination of nondeterministic and probabilistic behaviour with the possibility of termination, which is essential in program semantics. Our main contributions are presentation results for the monads, providing equational reasoning tools for establishing equivalences and distances of programs.
