Reid Barton (Jan 09 2021 at 17:42):

You should read intervals, not notes. It's easy to distinguish intervals visually up to a tenth or so--the lines and spaces give you the parity of the interval directly, so you only need to distinguish a handful of distances.

Reid Barton (Jan 09 2021 at 17:44):

It's much more important to distinguish fifths from sixths than Gs from As, because the whole musical system is symmetric in the pitch classes anyways.
