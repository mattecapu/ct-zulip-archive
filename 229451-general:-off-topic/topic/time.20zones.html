Definitely don't worry about it; one of the benefits of Zulip is the ability to do that in a reasonable way, without creating a huge wall of text that's hard to link back to past discussions, as you might have done on IRC. Cyphase (Jun 09 2020 at 11:32):

I think that's orthogonal to sending a lot of text to a single topic; but I also don't think that's necessarily bad at all, if you've got a lot to say. It's the content that decides spam-ness, not the quantity of text. And if it does become too much, or goes off topic, the messages can be moved to a different topic!
