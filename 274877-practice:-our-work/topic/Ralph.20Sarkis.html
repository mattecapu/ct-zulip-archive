Evan Patterson (Mar 07 2021 at 23:13):

One thing I like a lot about that book is that it works many examples of topos-theoretic constructions, in the concrete setting of presheaf categories where you can calculate things. The running examples include sets, graphs, reflexive graphs, and discrete dynamic systems (or "evolutive sets," as the authors call them).
